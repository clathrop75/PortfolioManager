Value Investing

The principles and formulas of value investing are powerful yet accessible to the casual investor. The data that is used to assess both a company and it's stock are freely available. So why aren't more people taking advantage of it? For one very simple reason - it requires stocks to be ranked. A judicious investor can gather and the apply the formulas to a few hundred stocks over the course of a year, but even that requires a time commitment that most people simply cannot make. Even still, a few hundred stocks is insufficient. We handle the hard part for you. We gather the data, you select the formulas and then we rank the stocks for you.
